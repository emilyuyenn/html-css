MY CHOSEN WEBSITE

The website that I chose to study is Pixabay

As a person who loves photography, I cannot don't mention about Pixabay. Pixabay is a popular online platform that provides high-quality images, illustrations, vector graphics, and videos that are available for free use. Users can download and use these assets for various purposes, including personal, commercial, and educational projects, without the need for attribution or payment. Pixabay is a valuable resource for designers, content creators, and anyone in need of visually appealing and legally usable media content.

Some advantage for users when using Pixabay:

  • Vast Library: Pixabay hosts a vast and diverse collection of images, vector graphics, illustrations, and videos contributed by a community of photographers and artists.
  • Free to Use: Most content on Pixabay is offered under a Creative Commons CC0 license, which means it can be used without attribution, even for commercial purposes.
  • Search and Filters: Users can search for specific content using keywords and apply filters to find the perfect asset for their project.
